

A 92-year-old man has been hospitalized following an elleged attack by a brick-wielding woman and several others in a brutal attack on that occurred on July 4, according to the Sacramento Bee.


Rodolfo Rodriguez accidentally bumped into a young woman while trying to pass her on the sidewalk near his home, and the child’s mother allegedly erupted by shoving him to the ground and smashing his head with a brick.


The horrific incident, which also included four men kicking and cursing the elderly man, was captured by a bystander who called the police.

Rodriguez suffered a broken cheekbone and two broken ribs. He remains hospitalized according to ABC7.com.
