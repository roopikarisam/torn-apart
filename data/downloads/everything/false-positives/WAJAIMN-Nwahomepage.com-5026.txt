FAYETTEVILLE, Ark. (KNWA) -- HAZMAT crews were called to the Washington County Detention Center on Friday after a mysterious envelope showed up. 


The Fayetteville Fire Department said the envelope contained an unknown substance. 


Officials described the material as a "crystal-like" substance found by the receptionist as she checked the mail. 


After testing the substance, officials determined it wasn't harmful.
