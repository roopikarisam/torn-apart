ORLANDO, Fla. (FOX 35 WOFL) - The two men accused of setting off acid bombs outside an Orange County home are coming clean to deputies.


Nathan Martin told investigators that his roommate wanted two people living on his property to move. So, they decided to make the bombs to scare them.


Both accused bombers are still in jail this morning.
