NASHVILLE — One day after escaping from jail in Macon County and being added to the Tennessee Bureau of Investigation’s Top 10 Most Wanted list, Dylan Cecil Ferguson surrendered Thursday morning to law enforcement in Trousdale County.


Ferguson, 20, had been wanted by the Macon County Sheriff’s Office and the TBI, after law enforcement said he escaped from the county’s jail Wednesday, along with two other individuals: Jeffrey Strong and Matt White.


More: One of three escaped Macon County jail inmates added to Tennessee's 'Most Wanted' list


As of Thursday afternoon Strong and White remained on the lam.


Earlier on Thursday morning, a family member helped coordinate Ferguson’s surrender, which occurred at the sheriff’s department in Hartsville, Tenn.
