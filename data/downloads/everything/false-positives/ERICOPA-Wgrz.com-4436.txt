LOCKPORT, N.Y. — A Lockport woman was arrested on driving while impaired charges under Leandra’s Law Friday evening after a two-car accident in the Town of Lockport.


According to the Niagara County Sheriff's Office, a vehicle driven by Andrea Lang, 34, was heading westbound on Robinson Road when it crossed over the double yellow line and struck an eastbound vehicle at about 5:57 p.m. Friday

The driver of the eastbound vehicle suffered injuries and was taken by ambulance to the Erie County Medical Center for treatment. Lang and her 5-year-old passenger were not injured.


Deputies performed sobriety tests on Lang at the scene and she was subsequently charged with aggravated vehicular assault, a Leandra’s Law violation, driving while ability impaired by drugs, driving while ability impaired by the combined influence drugs and alcohol, child endangerment, reckless driving and multiple Vehicle and traffic charges.


Lang is being held at the Niagara County Jail on $2,500 bail.


 Niagara County Sheriff's Office is continuing to investigate.
