MONTGOMERY, WV (WVNS) -- A man who was caught inappropriately touching himself at a gym in Fayette County is now in jail, according to deputies. 


The alleged incident happened on Monday, June 11 at a gym in Montgomery. 


Angel L. DeLeon-Morales is charged with indecent exposure. Witnesses told deputies they saw DeLeon-Morales enter the weight room and touch himself while watching women workout. He's now in jail.
