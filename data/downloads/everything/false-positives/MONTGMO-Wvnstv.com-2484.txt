GAULEY BRIDGE, W. Va. (WVNS) -- A woman in Fayette County has been arrested after deputies say she attacked her child with a tire iron.


Fayette County deputies were called to Montgomery General Hospital after a report of a mother attacking her 16-year-old son. It happened at their home in Gauley Bridge Monday night. 


Deputies arrested Michelle Saunders.She's charged with one felony count of child abuse resulting in injury. She's being held in the Southern Regional Jail on a $10,000 bond.
