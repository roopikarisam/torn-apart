Gallery: Police seek Washington Township gas station robber


    
        
            By Matt Gray

mgray@njadvancemedia.com,


For NJ.com

WASHINGTON TWP. -- Police have charged a Williamstown man in connection with an armed robbery at a Washington Township gas station.

Police shared security camera footage after a man carrying a crowbar robbed an attendant at the Gastrol station on the Black Horse Pike on Sept. 12.

Authorities announced Wednesday that they have charged Bertram C. Franks Jr., 49, with the crime.

Franks was charged wth robbery, possession of a weapon for an unlawful purpose and theft.

He was placed in Salem County Correctional Facility pending a hearing.
