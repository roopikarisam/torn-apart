
	RC Lens defender William Bianda has arrived in Italy and will have his medical with Roma tomorrow morning in a €5m move.


	The 18-year-old was greeted at the airport and held up a Giallorossi scarf for photographers.


	He is expected to have his medical at the Villa Stuart clinic in Rome on Thursday at 8am local time.


	Bianda came up through the Lens youth academy and is a centre-back with seven caps for France at Under-18 level.


	There is a five-year contract waiting for him at Trigoria.
