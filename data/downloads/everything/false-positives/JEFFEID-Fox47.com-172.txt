

MADISON, Wis. - A citizen contacted the Dane County 911 center after seeing an escaped inmate using a phone near a 7-Eleven convenient store, leading to the fugitive's arrest, police said.


Tyler M. Miller was taken into custody and returned to the Dane County jail, police said.


Miller, 28, of Stoughton had fled Saturday night through the back door of the William H. Ferris Center at 2120 Rimrock Road, police said.
