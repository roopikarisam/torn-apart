Gallery: N.J. week in crime: Nov. 3, 2017


    
        
            By Bill Gallo Jr.

bgallo@njadvancemedia.com

 For NJ.com

PENNS GROVE -- A man has been charged with sexually assaulting a girl twice in the same day, authorities say.

James A Moore, 38, of Penns Grove was arrested on Thursday in connection with the case, according to Penns Grove Chief of Police John T. Stranahan Sr.

Moore was charged with two counts of aggravated sexual assault, two counts of sexual assault, two counts of endangering the welfare of a child and trespassing, the chief said.

The alleged victim is a 14-year-old girl.

Moore allegedly assaulted the girl in a public park and inside a vacant building both in Penns Grove on Wednesday, according to Stranahan.

Moore remained in the Salem County Correctional Facility, Mannington Township, Friday night.
