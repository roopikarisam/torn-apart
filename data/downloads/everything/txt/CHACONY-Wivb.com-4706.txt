JAMESTOWN, N.Y. (WIVB) - A man charged with hanging a dog from a tree in Chautauqua County has been indicted by a grand jury.


Robert Overton Jr., 48, was accused of aggravated cruelty to animals -- a felony charge.


According to police and court documents, Overton hanged a family dog named "Champagne" in Jamestown in late November or early December.


The animal was seen by a person walking through the area of Hallock St. near Palmer St.


He had previously been released from custody, but Overton was remanded to the Chautauqua County Jail on $20,000 bail following his Monday arraignment.
