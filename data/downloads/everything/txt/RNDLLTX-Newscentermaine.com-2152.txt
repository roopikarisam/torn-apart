Update: Randall Lee Marsh has been located and taken into custody by Cumberland County Sheriff's Deputy. He has been taken to Cumberland County Jail, he will be arraigned.


LAKES REGION (NEWS CENTER Maine) -- The Cumberland County Sheriff's Office is warning people of an 'armed and dangerous' man who they say was last seen in Bridgton at 6:30 a.m. Friday.

They said 33-year-old Randall Lee Marsh of Bridgton is wanted on active arrest warrants in connection with a Domestic Violence Assault and Refusal to Submit to Arrest on Detention that occurred in Sebago, Maine on Tuesday. He was last seen wearing blue-jeans and a black hooded sweatshirt.


Marsh fled from Law Enforcement and eluded capture throughout the week.


Police believe Marsh has a handgun and said he has made statements that he is willing to take his own life or shoot at law enforcement if confronted. He is also wanted in connection with two residential burglaries.


Police described him as a white male, 5’03”, 160 lbs., with blonde hair and blue eyes.
