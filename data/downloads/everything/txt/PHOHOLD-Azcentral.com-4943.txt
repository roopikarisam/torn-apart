A man died Tuesday afternoon after jumping from the eighth floor of a parking structure in downtown Phoenix, police said.


Phoenix police shut down Jefferson Street between Third and Fourth avenues while officers investigated.


The parking structure is across the street from the Maricopa County Assessor's Office and the U.S. District Court for Arizona. 


Officers said they expected the street to be closed for at least three hours.
