Kids will be the big winners when the Dearborn Heights Fire Department battles the Dearborn Police Department for the most toys.
(Photo: Kurt Strazdins)

It’s being billed as the Battle of 2 Cities, but the winners will be the kids as the Dearborn Police take on the Dearborn Heights Fire Department on Wednesday.


Related:

To participate in the battle residents are asked to bring an unwrapped toy to the Dearborn Police Department, 16099 Michigan Ave., with the Dearborn Heights Fire Department asking residents to do the same, dropping them off at  4500 S. Telegraph Rd.


The city that collects the most toys will win the United States Marine Corps Toys for Tots championship trophy for the year.


All toys collected will be distributed to underprivileged children in Dearborn Heights and Dearborn.
