BUTLER COUNTY, OH (FOX19) – Corrections officers found an inmate unresponsive Monday morning at the Butler County Jail.


Billy Hall, 31, was found around 8 a.m. and transported to Fort Hamilton Hospital, where he was pronounced dead shortly after, according to Sheriff Richard Jones.


Take the news with you. Download the Cincinnati.com app.


Hall was being held on drug, theft, and unauthorized used of a vehicle charges out of Hamilton County.


The Butler County Detectives Division and Butler County Coroner’s Office are conducting the investigation. The cause of death has not yet been determined.
