GATE CITY, Va. – Three Tennessee women face charges in Scott County, Virginia, following a pursuit and shooting on Tuesday night, according to the Virginia State Police.


Elisabeth G. Lloyd, 26, was charged with attempting to elude law enforcement. Autumn L. Gillis, 28, was charged with one felony count of eluding law enforcement, one count of obstruction/resisting law enforcement, and reckless use of a firearm. Lauren D. Venable, 24, was charged with one felony count of eluding law enforcement. All three reside in Kingsport.


Gate City Police attempted to stop a Honda Accord on state Route 665 on Tuesday night, State Police said in a news release. A pursuit ensued during which time police said the occupants fired multiple shots at an officer’s vehicle, the release states. No officers were injured.


The three women are being held without bail at the Southwest Virginia Regional Jail in Duffield.
