Scott Allen Melton

A Crozet man was arrested Friday on multiple drug charges.

Albemarle County Police have charged Scott Allen Melton with five counts of possession with the intent to distribute a schedule I or II narcotic and possession with the intent to sell or distribute marijuana.

Police seized 28 pounds of marijuana, methamphetamine, LSD, powder and crack cocaine and THC extracts after responding to the 6700 block of Jarmans Gap Road for a reported narcotics violation.

Melton currently is being held at the Albemarle-Charlottesville Regional Jail.
