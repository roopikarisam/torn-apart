
    
        
            By Community Bulletin

The Salem County Sheriff's Office has announced the next Salem County TRIAD meeting will be held Wednesday, Dec. 13. This meeting will be TRIAD's holiday gathering.

The event will be held at the Salem County Fraternal Order of Police Hall, 693 South Broadway, Pennsville. The meeting will begin promptly at noon. 

Guest speaker will be Charles Clarkson, Esq., project director of the Jewish Family Services of Middlesex County. Clarkson will share information on how to protect yourself from Medicare/Medicaid fraud, waste and abuse. 

There will also be a demonstration of some senior self-defense tactics from Lt. Eduardo Avellanet of the Salem County Correctional Facility, as well as some safety tips for the holiday season. 

Lunch will immediately follow.

We will continue to take old/unused cell phones for donations and expired medication for proper disposal. 

The event is free, but those who plan to attend should RSVP to Cpl. Mario Fucci at 856-935-7510 Ext. 8595 by Dec. 11 To arrange transportation through the Salem County Office of Aging, please call 856-339-8627 no later than Dec. 11. 

Information provided by the Salem County Sheriff's Office.
