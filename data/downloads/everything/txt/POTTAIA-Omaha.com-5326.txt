COUNCIL BLUFFS — A Bellevue man has been charged with felony willful injury after police said he ran over a Crescent, Iowa, man.

Randall Maresch was being held at the Pottawattamie County Jail on Monday.

Council Bluffs police officers responded to an accident at 12:37 p.m. Saturday that police determined was a road rage incident in the parking lot of Family Fare. Maresch, 27, is accused of driving his vehicle toward the man, causing him to roll across the car’s hood before falling to the ground. The Crescent man was taken to an Omaha hospital and treated for injuries that were not life-threatening.
