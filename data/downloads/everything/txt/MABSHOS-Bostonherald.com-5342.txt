A Lynn man accused of pretending to be a cop during a string of crimes last weekend was ordered held at Bridgewater State Hospital while doctors determine if he is competent to stand trial.


Christian Alvarez, 33, pleaded not guilty to charges of impersonating a police officer, assault and battery with a dangerous weapon, armed robbery and several motor vehicle crimes after fleeing from cops. He is due back in court for a dangerousness hearing April 27.
