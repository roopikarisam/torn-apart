FAYETTEVILLE, Ark. (KNWA) - Fort Smith Police arrested the mother of an 18-month-old baby who was found dead in Fort Smith in January. 


According to Cpl. Anthony Rice,  after further review of the case, 20-year-old Sierra Johnson was arrested and faces charges with permitting the abuse of a minor. 


Police say the girl's body was found on January 30 and taken to a local hospital.


According to Cpl. Rice, the child had unexplained bruises on her body, however, the cause of death was unknown.  


Johnson's  boyfriend, 25-year-old Tyree Williams was arrested at the time for an unrelated case, but then was charged for first degree murder in the child's death.                


Johnson was taken to the  Sebastian County Adult Detention Center with a bond set at $7,500. 


She will appear in front of a court on February 21.
