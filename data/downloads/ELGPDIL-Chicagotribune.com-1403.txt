An Elgin man has been arrested on charges he pistol-whipped a man while robbing him, Elgin police said.


Cecil R. Palmer, 26, of the 3100 block of Friendship Street, was charged with one count of class X armed robbery with a firearm by the Cook County State's Attorney's Office, according to an Elgin Police Department news release.


Police were called at 11:43 a.m. Jan. 14 to the 700 block of Terrace Court about a holdup in which the victim was struck in the face before being robbed of undisclosed personal items, the release said.


The man, who knew Palmer and identified him to police, was treated for injuries that were not life-threatening.
