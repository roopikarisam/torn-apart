36 resorts, chef-owned restaurants for New Year's Day dinner in Phoenix

Photos: Restaurants for New Year's Eve dinner 2017

PHOENIX | Mowry & Cotton at The Phoenician | The restaurant's inaugural New Year’s Eve Porch Party will boast unlimited food and drinks ($95) along with live entertainment and dancing from 8 p.m. to 1 a.m. Nibble on passed bites and stroll up to food stations showcasing dishes like fire-braised lamb tacos, grilled pheasant and foie gras mousse. Reservations recommended. | Details: 6000 E. Camelback Road, Phoenix. 480-423-2530, mowryandcotton.com. The Phoenician

Photos: Restaurants for New Year's Eve dinner 2017

PHOENIX | Wright’s at The Biltmore | A luxurious five-course dinner ($195) features light bites like beet-cured scallop and tea-smoked ostrich. Other dishes include cast-iron duck breast with duck confit bread pudding; prime beef tenderloin with green-chile custard potato; and a white-chocolate raspberry pavé for dessert. A reception begins at 7 p.m., and the dinner will be served at 8. Reservations required. | Details: Arizona Biltmore, 2400 E. Missouri Ave., Phoenix. 602-955-6600, arizonabiltmore.com. Wright's at the Biltmore

Photos: Restaurants for New Year's Eve dinner 2017

PHOENIX | The Gladly and Citizen Public House | From 4 p.m. to midnight, The Gladly's three-course menu ($60) will feature options like kabocha squash, veal meatloaf, Oaxacan chile short rib, parsley-garlic chicken and black and tan pudding. At the same time, a different three-course menu ($60) will be served at its sister restaurant, Citizen Public House (their pan-seared scallops are pictured). Reservations recommended. | Details: The Gladly, 2201 E. Camelback Road, Phoenix. 602-759-8132, thegladly.com. Citizen Public House, 7111 E. Fifth Ave., Scottsdale. 480-398-4208, citizenpublichouse.com. Joanie Simon

Reservations required. | Details: , 5200 E. Camelback Road, Phoenix. 602-808-0766, royalpalmshotel.com. Courtesy of T. Cook's

Start with sweet-potato bisque or a salad and finish with a black-and-white cake with raspberry dark chocolate ganache. Dinner includes a champagne toast. Reservations recommended. | Details: Sheraton Grand Phoenix, 320 N. Third St., Phoenix. 602-817-5400, sheratonphoenixdowntown.com. Courtesy of District American Kitchen
