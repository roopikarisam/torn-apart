The average salary for a teacher in an Arizona school district last school yearwas $48,372, according to the annual report from the Arizona Office of the Auditor General.


According to the report, the average teacher has been in the profession for just less than 12 years.


In comparison, according to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, California teachers average $76,523 , Nevada teachers average $56,803, Utah teachers average $55,460 and New Mexico teachers average $56,613.
