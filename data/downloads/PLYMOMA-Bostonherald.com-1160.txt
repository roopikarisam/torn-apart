A Maine man accused of stabbing another man seven times in Bridgewater late Friday night is facing an attempted murder charge, police said.


Adam Martinez, 41, of York, Maine, was arrested by officers responding to a reported stabbing on South Street about 11:55 p.m., Bridgewater police Chief Christopher Delmonte said. When they arrived, Delmonte said, officers found the 40-year-old victim, whose name was not released, suffering from non-life-threatening stab wounds to his back, neck and arm.
