A Pima County corrections officer was arrested after transporting drugs into jail, officials said.


Martin Lopez, 27, was arrested on Feb. 28, 2018, on suspicion of transportation of narcotics and promoting prison contraband, the Pima County Sheriff's Department said.


Lopez was a corrections officer for Pima County jail for approximately 11 months, officials said.


After a week-long investigation initiated by a tip given to the Pima County Sheriff’s Department Office of Professional Standards, Lopez was taken into custody, officials said.


The investigation is ongoing, officials added.
