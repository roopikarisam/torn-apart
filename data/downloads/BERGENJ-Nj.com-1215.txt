
    
        
            By Michaelangelo Conte


                     The Jersey Journal
                    

JERSEY CITY -- A Union City man was arrested in North Bergen on Saturday where police say he was driving a car that was reported stolen in Weehawken a few days earlier.

Jerry Serrano, of the 4200 block of Bergen Turnpike, was charged with receiving stolen property regarded the 2016 Acura which was reported stolen on April 9, the criminal complaint says.

Serrano made his first appearance on the charge yesterday in Criminal Justice Reform Court in Jersey City yesterday via video link from Hudson County jail in Kearny.

At the hearing, the state moved to detain him through the course of his prosecution. A detention hearing is scheduled for Thursday before Hudson County Superior Court Judge Paul DePascale in the Hudson County Administration Building in Jersey City.
