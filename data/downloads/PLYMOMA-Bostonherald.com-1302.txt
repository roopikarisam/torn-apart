Two men arrested this weekend in Whitman will be arraigned today in Brockton District Court on drug trafficking charges, cops said.


Jon Vanrader Jr., 27, of Brockton and Reginald Sadm, 25, of New Bedford were arrested late Saturday night in Whitman, cops said.
  


Sadm was released yesterday on a $3,040 bail and Vanrader is being held at the Plymouth County Correctional Facility on $5,040 bail, Whitman cops said.
