
    
        
            By Patrick Villanova


                     The Jersey Journal
                    

The death of a 48-year-old woman at the medical ward inside Hudson County jail has been ruled a suicide after an "abundant amount of foreign material" was found inside her stomach, her autopsy report revealed.

Police have arrested and charged a 54-year-old city man with theft for stealing a baby Jesus from the St. Vincent de Paul Church nativity scene, authorities said.

Newly inaugurated Gov. Phil Murphy showed at least one thing in common with predecessor Chris Christie on Tuesday night: the ability to needle Giants and Jets fans.

 A Hoboken employee was arrested Friday after a weeks-long investigation into missing batteries from parking pay stations, authorities said.

In Sports, Hudson Catholic picked up a win at St. Peter's Prep. The North Bergen wrestling team won its fourth straight match.
