A 36-year-old woman was struck and killed Thursday morning while jogging in Ahwtaukee Foothills, and authorities have arrested the driver, police say.


Witnesses said the vehicle drove onto the sidewalk and hit a woman who was jogging at about 4:30 a.m. near Lakewood Parkway and Amberwood Drive.


The jogger, who police later identified as Martha Hilts, was taken to a hospital, where she died of her injuries, police said.


Police said the driver, identified as 25-year-old Shannon Scott, remained at the scene, where investigators determined that she was impaired.


Scott was arrested on suspicion of manslaughter and was booked into a Maricopa County jail.
